Background

In the golden era of the Kesultanan Melayu Melaka, the sultans ruled with wisdom and valor. Among them, Hang Tuah, the legendary warrior, was entrusted with the Sultan’s most secret missions.

One day, the Sultan received reports of a traitor trying to leak royal secrets. To safeguard the empire, the Sultan encoded a vital message containing the location of hidden royal treasures and strategic orders. Only Hang Tuah, with his knowledge of secret ciphers, could decode it.



The Challenge:

You have stumbled upon the ancient digital arkib of the Melaka Sultanate, containing a mysterious encrypted message. The message is stored in a strange code, known only to Hang Tuah himself.

Legend says:

“The secrets of the Sultanate are hidden behind an ancient cipher. Only the wise who understand the ways of Hang Tuah can read it.”

Your task is to decode the message.
